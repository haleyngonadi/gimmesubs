Here's a quick overview of all the things you can do:

  1. Home is where you'll find your personalised news feed of stories that are relevant to your interests.

  2. Reading List is a convenient place that remembers stories you've chosen to read at another time.

  3. Favourites contains all of the stories that you've starred.

  4. Settings is the place to change which topics you are subscribed to, as well as how you are notified about new stories.
